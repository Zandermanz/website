American Chop Suey

Text about this delicious recipe go here.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of pasta
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cans of condensed tomato soup
  • 2 cans of stewed tomatoes
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring salted water to a boil and cook pasta until tender or according to package directions.
  2. Brown ground beef over medium high heat.
  3. As the beef is almost done, add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add condensed tomato soup and canned stewed tomatoes to beef and simmer. If unavailable, you may substitute regular tomato soup with added sugar for the condensed tomato soup.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce and stir.
